The First Blog: Gratitude to My Community

In Gratitude...
‍
It's been a very long and fun-filled journey. A journey in the ocean of knowledge, where I surfed along those beautiful and cute playful waves. Those adorable and beautiful waves dragged me along with them deep into the ocean. This ocean was filled with purity, innocence and divinity. Yes, those waves are none other than my students.

It's only their love and fair sightedness which tapped onto the skills that lay dormant within me. Each time they grew, they forced me to grow professionally.
‍
My relationship with them is like a necklace, where the pearls and the thread are tightly interwoven. It is they who are responsible for increasing the professional experiences in my treasure trove.
‍
Now, it's been a decade and a half I have been in the education field, and I feel I have been fortunate enough to touch so many lives and so many hearts, and in return, they gave me caseloads of fame, faith and the love from their families.
‍
My career soared across the globe by a mere word of mouth. I started tutoring students from the ICSE, IGCSE and IB boards. I also bagged up opportunities to train individuals from the corporate world to some I fine-tuned communication skills. I have also worked with some students towards polishing their language etiquette and body language.

To some, I catered a complete Finishing school before they would step into the competitive world. I consider myself fortunate enough to get the opportunity to teach senior citizens who gave selfless Seva to the ISKCON temple in Chowpatty, Mumbai.
‍
This is my first blog on my website, entirely dedicated to gratitude to all my students and their parents. I remain indebted to each one of you—heaps of love.

Sapna Shah
30 April 2021

About Me
I offer my respectful obeisance unto my spiritual master, the deliver of the fallen souls. His mercy turns dumb into eloquent speakers and enables the lame to cross the mountains...

Sapna Shah believes behind every successful student there is a profound support of a selfless, passionate, enthusiastic and an ardent teacher. She has been in this profession for more than one and a half decades. She started her career as a nursery teacher, and kept moving on the professional rungs reaching up to teaching students to grade 12. She has an experience of home tutoring students from all elite ICSE, IGCSE and IB schools in Mumbai , Hongkong, Dubai, Delhi, Udupi, Haryana and Chandigarh. She also grooms students mastering their skills in  Language Etiquette, Social Etiquette and Body Language. She has not spared a single topic under the roof of English untouched, only proves her to be a versatile educationist. Now, she is elated to spill her treasures to many more students who are desirous to excel in their schools and in their careers.
