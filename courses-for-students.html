I offer my respectful obeisance unto my spiritual master, the deliver of the fallen souls. His mercy turns dumb into eloquent speakers and enables the lame to cross the mountains...

She has been in this profession for more than one and a half decades. Now, she is elated to spill her treasures to many more students who are desirous to excel in their schools and in their careers.
