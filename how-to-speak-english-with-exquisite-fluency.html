How To Speak English With Exquisite Fluency

IS THERE A SHORTCUT TO LEARN ENGLISH?
‍
The answer to this question is a flat ‘NO’.

‍
I do not provide shortcuts to learn English. In my opinion, English is a beautiful language, and it has to be relished. However, there are some rules and tricks that, once mastered, will speed up your journey, where learning English will become more pleasant and easy.


1)Build a comfort zone between you and the language.

Accept it with all its good and bad. Sometimes you may come across rules which may not be applicable- for eg
‍
A)You say one blouse and two blouses; one spouse and two spouses but one mouse and two mice
B) The word ‘Live’ could be read as
i)The live show on tv.
ii) I live by the sea.
‍
The above two sentences have the exact spelling but two different pronunciation and two different meanings.
These are not the only exceptions. There will be many such exceptions, do not make them your stumbling stones. The simple rule is to learn them and move on.

2)CAN YOU HAVE A FIXED TIME TO LEARN ENGLISH AND MASTER IT AS WELL?

Again my answer is a flat ‘NO’.
‍
Make learning English an ongoing process, unlike your other subjects. Submerge yourself in learning English by making it your topmost priority. Speak in English, read in English, write in English, think in English, and, if possible, inhale English. And you will see the difference racing up.

3)I have heard many people say, ‘English is a funny language.’

But I believe that English is a tricky language, and it can be tackled by familiarizing yourself with the tricks. Be a little manipulative while conversing. The first trick is to listen attentively and have a street bright attitude.

For example, while you are talking with someone, if you are attentive, you will notice quite a few of your answers are a part of the other person’s conversation.

E.g.,
Hey, would you like to join me for dinner tonight?
No, not tonight.
Is this looking good? Yes, it is.
Can you explain your plans?
‍
Now this kind of question or conversation can make you a little shaky. So here you must remember only you know that you cannot explain this, but the other person does not know. So be a little manipulative. Buy some time. Your answer should
I need time.
‍
Go home, prepare and work over it and then present your answer.
Let any such conversation mire your confidence and bring you down. It's only your confidence that will you keep you going.

Remember, if you fall, fall like a seed to germinate and not like a leaf to die. Keep your spirits high.
‍
