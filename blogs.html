About Me
I offer my respectful obeisance unto my spiritual master, the deliver of the fallen souls. His mercy turns dumb into eloquent speakers and enables the lame to cross the mountains...

Sapna Shah believes behind every successful student there is a profound support of a selfless, passionate, enthusiastic and an ardent teacher. She has been in this profession for more than one and a half decades. She started her career as a nursery teacher, and kept moving on the professional rungs reaching up to teaching students to grade 12. She has an experience of home tutoring students from all elite ICSE, IGCSE and IB schools in Mumbai , Hongkong, Dubai, Delhi, Udupi, Haryana and Chandigarh.
