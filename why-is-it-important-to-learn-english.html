Why is it important to learn English?

Speaking English gives you access to widen your horizon, starting from getting a job to increasing your social circle, entertainment, science, commercial, to name a few fields. The English Language is considered an international language.  It is the official language used in 53 countries and is used as lingua franca ( a mutually known language) worldwide.

ENGLISH IS A GLOBAL LANGUAGE
Statistics show that more than a quarter of the world’s population speaks in English. Hence, knowing the language helps you blend with people with ease.

ENGLISH CAN HELP YOU INCREASE YOUR NETWORKING
No man or woman is an island. We all are social animals, and we need to communicate with people, just not around us, but also with those around the world. And many we notice that in a group we have quite a few people who can converse in English. Hence to extend a conversation, we all need to speak in a language known to many, expanding our social circle.

PROMINENT LANGUAGE USED ON NETWORKING SITES
Almost all the essential contents for research or information on the net is in English. All tech companies have their base in English speaking countries. We are in a world where information is on the tip of your figure one click, and you know everything. However, for that quick access, one needs to know the language.
‍
GETTING ADMISSIONS IN UNIVERSITIES ABROAD
Education is the primary goal of one’s life; it not only gives you exposure to the world but also improves the quality of your life. And not able to receive this opportunity due to the barrier of not knowing the language will leave you with remorse.

JOB OPPORTUNITIES
What an employer seeks in his employee is how competent and well versed his employee is? He would surely want his employees to speak English fluently, which helps him bag up more business.

TRAVELLING
When travelling to different cities, states or countries, only knowing your native language will sometimes not allow you to enjoy your stay and disturb your visit.
‍
Hence, the English language reflects elegance and helps to connect with people across the globe. Learn to speak English with fluency with my training courses.
